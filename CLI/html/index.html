About the Project

Welcome!

Congratulations on starting the server through the CLI. Well done. You will now be able to access most of the commands through this web interface.

Here you will find a variety of links that will take you to various pages that I have created. I recommend keeping your developer console open during your stay, so that you can keep an eye on how things function behind the scenes.

Some of the features here don't work entirely as I hoped they would. Without a useState, I wasn't able to clear the forms when submitting their data, for example. But it works well enough for what I needed it to do.

I'd recommend starting by going to the Create an Account page and creating a new user token for yourself. Once that's finished, copy your token ID to your clipboard and go to the Account Confirmation page and confirm your account. You should notice your token update itself once you do this.
